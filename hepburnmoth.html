Hepburn Like You Never Saw Her Before – as a MOTH!

By Sher

Brazen Hussies Founder

This is what we need right now because.... Why not?

Yep, it’s Katherine Hepburn in a metallic moth suit designed by Walter Plunkett for her second film, “Christopher Strong" (1933).

How often does one even SEE a metallic moth suit, much less with Miss Hepburn wearing it? Well, now we have!

And it’s fabulous and gloriously unexpected.

“Christopher Strong” is actually a tragedy, an over-the-top melodrama. But who cares, really,

If it’s not exactly brilliant cinema when you have Hepburn as a famous woman pilot having an affair with a stuck-up member of Parliament - with no birth control, apparently (that's a plot hint).
